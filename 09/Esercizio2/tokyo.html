Tokyo is the capital of Japan, located in the Kantō region on the island of Honshū.
Although it is often referred to as a city, according to the Japanese administrative order,
it is a metropolis: today's Tokyo metropolitan area is the result of the merger of Tokyo
Prefecture and its capital, Tokyo, in 1943. the merger of the two bodies was suppressed and,
with the name Tōkyō, is today one of the forty-seven prefectures of Japan, as it is actually
a large area on which several independent cities are located together in a single conurbation.
Because in fact the Japanese government and the emperor of Japan reside in the district of
Chiyoda, Tokyo is commonly referred to as the capital of the nation.
